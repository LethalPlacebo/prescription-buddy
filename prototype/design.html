Product design

Prescription Buddy is built around trust, timeliness, and transparent savings.

User needs

1

Core user jobs

Find a medication quickly, understand what to take and when, avoid missed doses, and pay the lowest reliable price without extra research.

Interaction logic

2

Search: users discover medications and add them to a persistent list.

Track: each prescription exposes details, refill timing, and adherence status.

Remind: reminders adapt to schedule, meal timing, and caregiver backup.

Optimize: pricing compares nearby and mail-order pharmacies with savings guidance.
